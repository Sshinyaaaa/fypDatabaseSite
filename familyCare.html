Family Care

Family care is a comprehensive approach to healthcare that focuses on the well-being of individuals within the context of their family and community. It emphasizes preventive care, early intervention, and the management of acute and chronic health conditions for all family members, regardless of age or gender. The goal of family care is to provide continuous and coordinated care that supports the overall health and well-being of the family unit.
