What treatments do we do?

Our clinic mainly focuses on primary care which cover a wide range of routine and preventive healthcare services. For more information, click on the services tab.

Why book an appointment?

Waiting time on appointments are significantly reduced. But we don't mind walk-ins as well, if that is what you prefer.

Simply call the customer service and provide the name of which you made the appointment on, then just relay whether a cancellation or reschedule is on due.

Just click on the contact us tab and send us a message!
